Suicides Rate Analysis Between 1985-2016

Introduction

Currently, there are almost 800,000 people giving up their own life every year, which means that thereâ€™s one person every 40 seconds. To get a comprehensive understanding on the factors and current trends, a visualized analysis of suicide rate in the worldwide is proposed in this project. The primary objective of this project is to visualize the suicide rates to find out the factors of suicides and trends among different continents and countries. In this project, we will select the top 10 countries to analyze Factors such as age, gender, generation and GDP (GDP per capita) for each country will be explored to find the relationship with suicide. Data visualization analysis has been completed by Tableau.



Data Description

The statistical data from this project comes from Kaggle. This dataset contains suicide rate among the world from 1985 to 2016 and these data were pulled from World Bank, United Nations Development Program and World Health Organization.
